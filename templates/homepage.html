Introducing AsianBot

Somtimes, students just need a push to do their homework. The classic Asian parent is the perfect example of how to apply this push. In a classic Asian household, students who do not complete their homework, or get a B will receive punishment. For students who do not have access to these inspirational Asian Parents, AsianBot attempts to act as a virtual one. Armed with state-of-the-art features like "automatic email home," AsianBot is guaranteed to help you succeed.
